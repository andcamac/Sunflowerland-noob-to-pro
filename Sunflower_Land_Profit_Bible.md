## 🌍 Seasonal Strategies

### 🌸 Spring Week (7 Days)

**Strategy:** Crop yield maximization

- **Plant**: Carrots, Cabbages, Beetroots, Beans
- **Fruits**: Blueberries, Oranges (use Short Pickings: 2x speed!)
- **Equip**: Blossom Ward (+1 crop output)

**Watch for:**

- ⚠️ Tsunami days: Don't plant (or use Mangrove)
- ✅ Bountiful Harvest: Plant ALL plots, harvest 1.5-2x
- ✅ Sunshower: 2x growth = double cycles per day

> 💪 **Spring Power Move:** Sprout Mix (+0.2) + Bee Swarm (+0.2) + Blossom Ward (+1) + Bountiful Harvest (+1) = each carrot plot yields 3-4 carrots!

---

### ☀️ Summer Week (7 Days)

**Strategy:** Eggplant farming + Animal focus

- **Plant**: Eggplant, Corn, Radish, Pepper
- **Fruits**: Tomatoes, Lemons (use Zesty Vibes)
- **Equip**: Solflare Aegis (-50% crop growth time!)

**Watch for:**

- ⚠️ Tornado: Place Pinwheel 1 day before
- ⚠️ Insect Plague: Place Bee Box
- 🌕 Full Moon: Eggplants instantly mature + Marine Marvel chance

> 💪 **Summer Power Move:** Buy 3 Moon Berry seeds during Full Moon for unique fruits → Lava Pit (Volcano) → Lunar Doll crafting

---

### 🍂 Autumn Week (7 Days)

**Strategy:** Apple/Banana fruit farming

- **Plant**: Pumpkin, Yam, Broccoli, Onion, Artichoke
- **Fruits**: Apples, Bananas (use Long Pickings: 2x speed!)
- **Equip**: Autumn's Embrace (-50% growth time)

**Watch for:**

- ✅ Bountiful Harvest: ESPECIALLY valuable (advanced crops +1)
- ⚠️ Tornado: Same protection as summer

> 💪 **Autumn Power Move:** Most +1 crop bonuses of any season - max yield on long-cycle Artichoke/Broccoli (highest XP/coin)

---

### ❄️ Winter Week (7 Days)

**Strategy:** Defensive play + cooking focus

- **Plant**: Cauliflower, Parsnip, Rhubarb, Kale, Turnip
- **Fruits**: Tomatoes, Lemons (year-round)
- **Equip**: Frozen Heart (+1 crop output)

**Watch for:**

- ⚠️ Great Freeze: Place Heater (or Winter Guardian)
- ✅ Bountiful Harvest: Long winter crops benefit MORE

> 💪 **Winter Power Move:** Slower outdoor crops = perfect for cooking backlog, composter processing, deep mining/oil drilling, greenhouse focus
